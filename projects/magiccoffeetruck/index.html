About Magic Coffee Truck

Magic Coffee Truck is an Atlanta-based start-up food truck project that aims to:

  • Deliver a mobile coffee shop experience
  • Partner with local coffee roasters to feature tasty brews
  • Savor fantastic creations of talented baristas
  • Use green/sustainable resources
  • Provide coffee education because science is magic

As we get more wheels on the ground, you'll be able to book Mobile Coffee Truck for groovy parties, corporate events, or regional events. (Everyone needs a peanut butter mocha with their funnel cake at the farmers market.) It's our dream to be the first coffee thought and afternoon coffee break for folks everywhere.
